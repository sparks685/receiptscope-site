ReceiptScope

ReceiptScope is a web-based receipt scanner and expense export tool for freelancers and small businesses.

What we offer

  • Scan receipts from images
  • Extract structured expense data
  • Export expenses to CSV

How it works

  1. Upload a receipt image or PDF
  2. ReceiptScope extracts merchant, date, totals, tax, and line items
  3. Export your expenses to CSV for accounting or tax purposes

ReceiptScope offers a free plan with optional paid upgrades for higher usage.
